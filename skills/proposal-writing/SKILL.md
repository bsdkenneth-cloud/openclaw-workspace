# Proposal Writing Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- proposal drafting
- scope of work writing
- service package structure
- value proposition articulation
- commercial writeups
- client-facing business documents

## Rules
- Be clear about scope, deliverables, assumptions, and exclusions.
- Prefer persuasive logic over vague claims.
- Separate proposal content from negotiation points.
- Keep writing decision-oriented and commercially grounded.

## Output Style
- proposal summary
- scope of work
- deliverables list
- commercial recommendations
