# Customer Service Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- customer replies
- complaint handling
- service recovery messaging
- expectation management
- after-sales communication
- support workflow wording

## Rules
- Stay calm, clear, and professional.
- Acknowledge the issue without escalating it.
- Separate apology, clarification, solution, and next step.
- Prefer trust-rebuilding language over defensiveness.

## Output Style
- reply draft
- service recovery message
- clarification message
- next-step guidance
