# Market Trend Research Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- market trend scanning
- product trend analysis
- industry trend analysis
- regional demand comparison
- customer demand shifts
- strategic trend summaries

## Core Method
Before giving trend conclusions:
1. Define the exact market, product, or topic.
2. Expand it into related search terms.
3. Check current public signals across multiple source types.
4. Separate long-term trends from short-term spikes.
5. Compare region, buyer type, and timing where relevant.
6. Highlight what appears to be increasing, stable, weakening, or uncertain.
7. Re-check whether the trend signal is strong enough to support a business decision.

## Rules
- Prefer current public signals over static assumptions.
- Separate evidence from inference.
- Mark weak signals clearly.
- Distinguish hype, seasonal movement, and structural demand.
- Prefer strategy-ready summaries over generic commentary.

## Output Style
- topic definition
- expanded keyword set
- current signals
- trend direction
- structural vs short-term analysis
- business implications
- uncertainty notes
