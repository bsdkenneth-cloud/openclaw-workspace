# Social Publishing Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- social post drafting
- caption optimization
- platform-specific adaptation
- CTA refinement
- post sequencing
- publishing coordination

## Rules
- Adapt tone to platform and audience.
- Keep outputs post-ready and easy to publish.
- Separate caption, hook, CTA, and hashtags.
- Prefer clarity and engagement over fluff.

## Output Style
- platform-ready caption
- hook options
- CTA options
- hashtag set
