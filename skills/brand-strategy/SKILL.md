# Brand Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- brand positioning
- audience definition
- value proposition design
- messaging architecture
- differentiation strategy
- brand growth direction

## Rules
- Start from the real customer problem and market position.
- Separate brand identity, offer logic, audience, and messaging.
- Prefer strategic clarity over decorative branding language.
- Make assumptions, risks, and tradeoffs explicit.

## Output Style
- brand strategy summary
- positioning statement
- audience definition
- messaging framework
