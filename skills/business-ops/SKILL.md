# Business Ops Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- operating plans
- SOP drafting
- execution checklists
- role and responsibility design
- business workflow structuring
- management summaries

## Rules
- Prefer structured outputs.
- Separate facts, assumptions, risks, and actions.
- Do not invent numbers without marking them as estimates.
- Keep outputs decision-oriented.

## Output Style
- executive summary
- action list
- risks
- next-step recommendations
