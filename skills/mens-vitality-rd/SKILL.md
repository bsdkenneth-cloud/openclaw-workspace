# Men's Vitality R&D Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- men's vitality product concept development
- ingredient-direction analysis
- performance and wellness positioning

- category research support

## Rules

- Separate concept, ingredient role, positioning, and product claim.
- Prefer practical product-development logic over vague marketing language.
- Make assumptions, limitations, and uncertainties explicit.

## Output Style
