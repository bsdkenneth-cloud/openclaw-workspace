# Pet Food R&D Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- pet food concept development
- ingredient logic review
- nutrition positioning
- formulation direction
- product differentiation planning
- pet health-oriented product design

## Rules
- Start from the target pet need and intended outcome.
- Separate ingredient purpose, nutritional role, and product claim.
- Prefer practical formulation logic over vague health marketing.
- Make assumptions, limitations, and uncertainties explicit.

## Output Style
- product concept summary
- ingredient rationale
- formulation direction
- product positioning notes
