# Research Intelligence Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- market research
- competitor analysis
- trend scanning
- commercial intelligence gathering
- decision support summaries
- structured comparison work

## Rules
- Distinguish facts from inference.
- Prefer source-grounded reasoning.
- Summarize findings into business implications.
- Highlight uncertainty, gaps, and risks clearly.

## Output Style
- research summary
- comparison table
- implications
- risks
- recommended next actions
