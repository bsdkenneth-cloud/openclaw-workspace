# Investment Research Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- investment direction analysis
- strategy research
- thesis building
- scenario comparison
- market entry and timing review
- decision-support summaries

## Core Method
Before giving investment suggestions:
1. Define the actual asset, industry, business, or opportunity.
2. Identify the decision horizon and risk tolerance.
3. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions and narratives.
4. Link trend signals to possible investment directions.
5. Compare upside, downside, and uncertainty.
6. Re-check whether the recommendation is based on real evidence rather than hype.

## Rules
- Start from first principles and current market context.
- Separate research, inference, and recommendation.
- Prefer scenario analysis over one-direction certainty.
- Make uncertainty and decision conditions explicit.
- Avoid presenting speculation as fact.
- Prefer actionable research framing over vague commentary.

## Output Style
- opportunity definition
- thesis summary
- supporting signals
- scenarios
- upside and downside
- uncertainty notes
- suggested next step
