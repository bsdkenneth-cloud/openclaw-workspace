# Request Template

Use this input structure before running lead discovery:

- target_market:
- buyer_type:
- core_keyword:
- related_keywords:
- region:
- country_focus:
- output_goal:
- required_fields:
- exclude_terms:
- notes:

## Example
- target_market: maritime shipping companies
- buyer_type: tanker owners and operators
- core_keyword: tanker company
- related_keywords: tanker owner, tanker operator, ship management company
- region: global
- country_focus: Singapore, Greece, UAE
- output_goal: clean lead list for outreach
- required_fields: company_name, website, contact_page, public_email, public_phone, country
- exclude_terms: job boards, news articles, vessel tracking only
- notes: prioritize official websites and verified business contact pages
