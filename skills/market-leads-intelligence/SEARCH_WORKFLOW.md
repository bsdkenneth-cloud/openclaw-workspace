# Search Workflow

## Execution Sequence
1. Define target market and buyer type.
2. Expand the original keyword into related commercial search terms.
3. Search official websites, contact pages, directories, trade associations, and public company profiles.
4. Capture raw findings before cleaning.
5. Extract only public business contact details.
6. Separate verified data from unverified data.
7. Deduplicate companies and contact entries.
8. Structure results using OUTPUT_TEMPLATE.md.
9. Mark missing fields clearly.
10. Recommend next search angles if coverage is weak.

## Keyword Expansion Logic
For each core keyword, expand into:
- industry synonyms
- buyer-type terms
- regional terms
- official directory terms
- association terms
- contact-intent terms

## Example Expansion
Core keyword: tanker company

Expand into:
- tanker owner
- tanker operator
- shipping company
- marine transport company
- tanker fleet operator
- ship management company
- tanker chartering company
- tanker company contact
- tanker company email
- tanker company official website

## Source Priority
1. Official company websites
2. Official contact pages
3. Industry directories
4. Trade associations
5. Public LinkedIn company pages
6. Public business listings

## Output Rule
Always produce:
- raw findings
- cleaned lead table
- missing-data notes
- next-step search recommendations
