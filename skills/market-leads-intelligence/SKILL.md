# Market Leads Intelligence Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- keyword-based market lead discovery
- related keyword expansion
- public company list collection
- public contact detail extraction
- lead deduplication and classification
- table-ready output preparation

## Core Method
Before collecting leads:
1. Identify the real target market.
2. Identify the real buyer type.
3. Expand the keyword set into related search terms.
4. Search the open web for relevant companies, directories, associations, and official websites.
5. Extract only public business contact data.
6. Separate raw findings from verified findings.
7. Deduplicate, classify, and structure the results into a clean lead list.
8. Re-check whether the output is solving the real lead-generation problem.

## Rules
- Start from first principles and the true commercial objective.
- Use keyword expansion before searching.
- Prefer official websites, public contact pages, industry directories, and association lists.
- Extract only public business information.
- Separate company name, website, contact page, phone, email, country, and notes.
- Mark unclear or unverified fields clearly.
- Prefer structured table-ready output over narrative text.
- Avoid duplicate companies and duplicate contact entries.

## Output Style
- target market
- keyword set
- search logic
- raw lead findings
- cleaned lead table
- missing-data notes
- next-step recommendations
