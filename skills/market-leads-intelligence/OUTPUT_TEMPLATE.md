# Lead Output Table Template

Use this table structure for final output:

| company_name | industry | country | city | website | contact_page | public_email | public_phone | linkedin | source_url | keyword_found | confidence | notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Field Rules
- company_name: official company name
- industry: shipping, tanker, logistics, ship repair, etc.
- country: company country
- city: city if public
- website: official website
- contact_page: official contact page URL
- public_email: only public business email
- public_phone: only public business phone
- linkedin: public company LinkedIn if available
- source_url: source page used
- keyword_found: the keyword that led to the result
- confidence: high / medium / low
- notes: missing fields, ambiguity, or relevance notes
