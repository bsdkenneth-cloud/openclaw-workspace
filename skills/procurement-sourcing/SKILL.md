# Procurement Sourcing Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- supplier sourcing
- RFQ preparation
- quotation comparison
- sourcing workflow design
- purchasing communication
- vendor shortlist creation

## Rules
- Keep commercial comparisons explicit.
- Separate requirements, constraints, and supplier options.
- Do not present assumptions as confirmed specs.
- Prefer practical sourcing outputs over generic advice.

## Output Style
- sourcing brief
- supplier comparison
- RFQ structure
- purchasing recommendations
