# Compliance Risk Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- compliance review
- risk flagging
- red-flag detection
- boundary-setting in deals
- business risk reminders
- screening unclear or unsafe requests

## Rules
- Separate legal, commercial, operational, and reputational risk.
- Flag uncertainty clearly.
- Do not present risky assumptions as safe facts.
- Prefer caution where information is incomplete.

## Output Style
- risk summary
- red flags
- uncertainty notes
- safer next-step recommendations
