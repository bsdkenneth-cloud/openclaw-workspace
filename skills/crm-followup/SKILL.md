# CRM Follow Up Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- lead follow-up planning
- contact sequencing
- reminder drafting
- pipeline touchpoint design
- relationship maintenance
- conversion follow-up workflows

## Rules
- Keep follow-up timing and purpose explicit.
- Separate warm leads, cold leads, and active negotiations.
- Avoid repetitive or pushy wording.
- Prefer concise follow-up with clear next actions.

## Output Style
- follow-up draft
- contact sequence
- reminder structure
- pipeline action notes
