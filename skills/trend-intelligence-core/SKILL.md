# Trend Intelligence Core Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill before strategy, investment, business development, or market execution tasks.
Its job is to force current-trend analysis before decisions are made.

## Core Method
Before making recommendations, always:
1. Define the actual market or business topic.
2. Identify the target audience, buyer type, or investment target.
3. Expand the main topic into related search and trend keywords.
4. Scan current public signals, trend indicators, and recent developments.
5. Separate long-term structural trends from short-term hype.
6. Identify demand-side, supply-side, and sentiment-side signals.
7. Re-check whether the recommendation fits the actual direction of the market.
8. If trend data is weak or mixed, flag uncertainty clearly.

## Rules
- Do not rely only on static knowledge.
- Always prefer current trend signals where possible.
- Separate durable trends from temporary noise.
- Use trend analysis before giving investment or operating suggestions.
- Revisit trend assumptions when new information appears.
- When combined with other skills, this skill acts as an upper-layer decision filter.

## Output Style
- topic definition
- expanded keyword set
- current trend signals
- structural trend vs short-term noise
- implications
- uncertainty notes
- recommended next action
