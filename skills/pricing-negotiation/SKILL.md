# Pricing Negotiation Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- price discussion support
- counteroffer drafting
- margin protection logic
- negotiation structure
- commercial term framing
- deal positioning

## Rules
- Keep pricing logic explicit.
- Separate target price, walk-away point, and negotiable terms.
- Avoid emotional or vague bargaining language.
- Prefer commercially disciplined wording.

## Output Style
- pricing summary
- negotiation draft
- counteroffer options
- commercial position notes
