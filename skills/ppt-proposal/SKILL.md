# PPT Proposal Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- pitch deck planning
- proposal writing
- slide-by-slide structure
- speaker notes drafting
- executive presentation framing
- business narrative development

## Rules
- Keep the deck logic sequential and persuasive.
- Prefer concise, presentation-ready wording.
- Separate slide content from speaker notes.
- Focus on decision-making and business clarity.

## Output Style
- deck outline
- slide content
- speaker notes
- proposal summary
