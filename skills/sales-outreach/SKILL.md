# Sales Outreach Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- cold outreach drafting
- buyer qualification
- follow-up message design
- negotiation message structure
- professional business communication
- conversion-oriented outreach flows

## Rules
- Be professional, direct, and commercially aware.
- Protect credibility and avoid hype.
- Separate prospecting, qualification, and closing.
- Prefer concise language with clear next actions.

## Output Style
- outreach draft
- qualification questions
- follow-up sequence
- negotiation messaging
