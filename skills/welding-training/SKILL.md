# Welding Training Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- welding course design
- welder training structure
- metallurgy-aware instruction
- process discipline coaching
- qualification pathway planning
- practical training summaries

## Rules
- Keep technical instruction practical and explicit.
- Separate theory, demonstration, practice, and assessment.
- Avoid oversimplifying safety or metallurgy constraints.
- Prefer training outputs that can be executed in the field.

## Output Style
- training outline
- lesson structure
- competency goals
- assessment notes
