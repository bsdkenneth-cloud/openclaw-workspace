# Investor Deck Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- investor pitch structure
- fundraising narrative design
- growth story framing
- capital use explanation
- traction presentation
- investor-facing deck logic

## Rules
- Keep the narrative commercially credible.
- Separate current facts, assumptions, and future upside.
- Avoid hype without support.
- Focus on clarity, scalability, and investability.

## Output Style
- investor deck outline
- fundraising narrative
- capital use summary
- investor positioning notes
