# Translation Localization Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- translation drafting
- tone adaptation
- business localization
- bilingual messaging
- market-specific wording
- cross-language communication support

## Rules
- Preserve meaning before style.
- Adapt tone to audience and context.
- Separate literal translation from polished business phrasing when needed.
- Avoid awkward direct translation when localization is better.

## Output Style
- direct translation
- polished version
- tone notes
- localization recommendations
