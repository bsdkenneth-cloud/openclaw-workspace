# Fuel Trading Skill

## Purpose
Use this skill for:
- fuel trade support
- pricing basis discussion
- buyer and seller coordination
- deal term clarification
- offer and counteroffer structure
- commercial petroleum communication

## Rules
- Keep pricing basis and commercial terms explicit.
- Separate firm terms, indicative terms, and assumptions.
- Avoid vague price talk without structure.
- Prefer market-style disciplined wording.

## Output Style
- trade summary
- pricing structure notes
- negotiation draft
- commercial risk points
